Rich! One of my favorite crossworld people, so deserving of the 2023 Merl Reagle MEmorRiaL Award. He recently retired as the editor of the LAT crossword, which has freed up time for him … to get back into constructing!

So much for any chance of ever passing him in the most-published list. Someone give him a job, so he's too busy again!

An unusual grid design, featuring chunky blocks in the center, tiptoeing the line between not enough grid flow and ease of filling. It's a great way to experiment with nearly quad-stack entries in the lower left and upper right. Typically, achieving color and cleanliness out of all four entries would be extremely difficult, but segmenting the grid in this way helps a ton.

Excellent results. DIET PILLS / IN TOO DEEP + ROAD RACER / GOLDEN ERA / OKEY DOKEY … plus the delightful connection between SCHROEDER and TOY PIANO?

It's quite possible that Rich might have made a themeless or 133 before.

The grid put up a Saturday fight, dekeing me with HACEK (not to be confused with the NHLER Hasek), popping THEPO (that's the river Po, not THE PO-PO), and juking me out with Joel MCCREA, who was well before my time.

I like an easier-breezier solve on my Friday, and there's probably a reason why 85% of Rich's themelesses have run on a Saturday. It's so well-timed, though, scheduling this one the same day the ACPT starts, and Rich gets an honor he truly deserves. When I was asked if I wanted to nominate someone for the award, Rich's kindness and generosity over the years made it a no-brainer.
