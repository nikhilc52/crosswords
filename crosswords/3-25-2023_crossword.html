I USEd THE FORCE in solving another gem from Robyn, the themeless JEDI MASTER! So many conversational phrases perked me up — NO PRESSURE, STAND ASIDE … YOU DID WHAT?!

BONUS POINTS will be awarded to the best short story linking those three evocative phrases.

Y yes, I do love the FLUX CAPACITOR! I have such a vivid memory of seeing "Back to the Future" on the big screen, captivated by this amazing piece of technology. There hasn't been such a creative coinage ever since. I did get some glazed expressions from solvers when I used this a while back — give me a second to get up to 88 MPH so I can send similar questions back to that day.

Given Robyn and my love of all things fantastically awesome, I'm disappointed that EASTER EGGS wasn't clued to Ready Player One. Then again, Ready Player Two was Game Over. (Don't bother with it.)

CAN I SEE SOME ID? Didn't you just ask me that? Although the two entries aren't exactly the same, it stinks to get scooped by just a few weeks — especially when the previous clue was so wickedly clever! [Common request at a bar] does misdirect to drink orders, but playing on the "utterance" vs. "queue" definitions of "line" is hard to beat.

NO LEFT TURN is a decent enough entry. That clue, though … jughandle? Why is it called — ooh, super descriptive! I'll pour one out for learning that tidbit.

Robyn continues to flex some serious BIONICS in her themelesses.
