Jeff Chen notes:
It's difficult to stand out as a themeless grid technician. So many people are making them, and so few 72- or 70-word grids are ... read more

It's difficult to stand out as a themeless grid technician. So many people are making them, and so few 72- or 70-word grids are distinguishable from others. For any random themeless, you could give me twenty guesses, and I might not get the first letter of the person's first name.

Ryan is different. You ask me to name the five best low-word-count themeless grid builders, he's easily in there. Maybe top three.

Maybe top one.

Another beautiful football-shaped center adds to his distinguished portfolio. Not just five long Acrosses in this gigantic middle — seven (!) of them.

And that's not all. Sectioning off the middle would make it much more tractable. Many top-notch constructors have resorted to this. Ryan not only kept everything wide open, but he wove six (!) long Downs through — including sparklers like JET SETTERS and SOLO ARTIST! All of that with amazing smoothness. Not even a hint of an ERROR MESSAGE.

Incredible cluing, too, both in quality and quantity. [Go to the next line] made me think of how many times I've switched checkout lines, only to realize the ERROR MESSAGE flashing in my head. If only line management were as easy as PRESSing ENTER on my keyboard.

[One all over the board] cleverly sums everything up today. Anyone would be lucky to find themselves with Ryan as a TEACHER.
