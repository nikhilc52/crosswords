Patrick Berry crosswords are like gossamer, and one doesn't dissect gossamer. I have just a few thoughts about this deliciously smooth puzzle.

Such a lascivious NW corner, with TAKE A LOOK as the meat in an EXECRABLE STUD FARM sandwich. Thankfully, there's WISK to clean up the corner. TAKE A LOOK can also go G-rated, as in TAKE A LOOK... it's in a book... A Reading Rainbow. Children of the '80s, was there anything better than that butterfly in the sky? LeVar Burton had a hand in encouraging the creation of one of 2014's most moving books, the graphic novel "Harlem Hellfighters", by Max Brooks. It's a lightly fictionalized account of an African-American infantry regiment during WWI.


I'm not familiar with the sun god Inti, so INCAN didn't come easily to me. I'd like to think that someday it might be clued as "Flight beverage option?"
