Pikachu, I CHOOSE YOU! I will be mocked for my delight in watching "Pokemon" with my kids, and I choose to ignore their select words muttered at me when I try to teach them all the scientific intricacies of Pokemon evolution. I also choose [Select words?] as the best clue of this puzzle!

CUTAWAY GAG sounded so familiar, yet I couldn't figure out exactly what it meant. Curious that the Goog links it strongly to Family Guy. I always watched the show before I had kids, and I might have to consider returning to it after watching some of these clips. If I had remembered this TV trope, Malaika and Erik's clue would have slayed, but a more definitional clue served this family guy better.

WHITEY ON THE MOON is an interesting central marquee. You'd think that as a diehard engineer and scientist, I'd be gung-ho over space exploration, but it's always felt like a waste of resources. I've had so many discussions over the decades, including some with Jim Horne, none of which have swayed me, even given all the clear technological benefits that space travel has fostered. So I read the lyrics to WHITEY ON THE MOON eagerly, nodding along as I went.

I want my crossword to be pure escapist entertainment. I don't like being reminded that I got singled out and harassed at the grocery store last week by a white checker who thought I was a gang member stealing stuff. Still, I've come to realize that there are many different kinds of solvers out there who want very different things out of their puzzle, including IT'S SERIOUS topics.
