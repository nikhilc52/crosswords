Here's what OPPOSITES ATTRACT means in the italicized theme answers here:

18-Across: [Highway crossing] would normally be OVERPASS.

19-Across: [Fail] would normally be GO UNDER.

The opposites are OVER and UNDER and they attract each other magnetically, resulting in PASSOVER and UNDERGO. The rearranged words after attraction are also legitimate crossword entries — an elegant touch. I "fixed up" the answers below the grid so they correspond to their post-attraction state, and therefore fit the clues.
