I live in Seattle with my wife, Zhou, and our three kids, and I've become obsessed with running along a beautiful lake near our house. (I have a goal to run every day this year.) Sometime last year, I decided to ditch my earphones and be more present for the views of Mount Rainier, the beautiful sunrises, and, of course, splashing through puddles during the many, many days of rain. The side effect of that decision is that I think about crossword ideas. A lot. This one came to me at the end of a tiring run where I was imagining the Rocky theme music to pump myself up. Although "I OF THE TIGER" didn't make the final cut, I had lots of fun brainstorming this theme set.

When I'm not "runstorming" crossword themes or chauffeuring my kids around town, I spend a lot of time at the climbing gym, where I've met some of the most important people in my life. So, I'm grateful to the editors for keeping the clues for 70A and 56D as a nod to that community.
