Spencer Leach notes:
There's a somewhat common phenomenon in the crossworld where you receive the final copy of your puzzle about three weeks before it ... read more

There's a somewhat common phenomenon in the crossworld where you receive the final copy of your puzzle about three weeks before it runs (usually about a year after you last saw the grid) and you can't help but be a bit… underwhelmed. After all, you've improved your crossword-making skills a lot in the last year, and the puzzle sitting in front of you might not be up to your current crossword standards.

Safe to say, that was not the case here! I think this puzzle holds up just as well as it did the day I wrote it. I started out with my clue/answer pair for GEOCENTRIST (did anyone try egocentrist?), and the hits never stopped! Clues like [She's so full of herself!] or the devilish [Out] popped into my head like flashes of lightning one after the other until I was staring at a completed grid.

P.S. I played PICKLEBALL for the first time (after giving it an admittedly unflattering clue) and decided it is simply more fun than tennis or ping pong. Screw the haters.
