I am a child therapist who provides in-school mental health support to elementary and middle school students. I live in Colorado but am a Minnesotan at heart.

I am thrilled to have PADIDDLE as the seed for my debut New York Times puzzle! Last fall, as I was driving my six-year-old to school, I saw a car with only one headlight. Of course, I immediately thought, "PADIDDLE!" and then decided that would be a fun seed for a themeless. The clue for GO DEEP is a nod to my day job, and I'm happy to present what I think(?) is a new angle for that entry!
