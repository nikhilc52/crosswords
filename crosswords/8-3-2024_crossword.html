Rich Norris is a veteran constructor. He edited the Los Angeles Times crossword for years, and still had time to create nearly 200 puzzles for the New York Times. He has more Saturday puzzles than any other constructor, and it's not even close.

XWord Info has long tracked the youngest known constructors, but now thanks to some great work by Flip Koski, I also have an oldest known constructors page. That is, oldest at the age of their most recent published NYT puzzle. Mr. Norris can creep further up that list with each new published puzzle. If you should be on this list, or have information about who should be, please let me know.
