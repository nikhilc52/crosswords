Erica Hsiung Wojcik notes:
This puzzle's grid was based on a themeless by Kameron Austin Collins. I loved the intersecting 11s and the grid flow from a solver's ... read more

This puzzle's grid was based on a themeless by Kameron Austin Collins. I loved the intersecting 11s and the grid flow from a solver's perspective. I'm happy with the mix of entries I got into that center area.

Fun fact: I debuted the singular of 21D a few weeks ago, and here I am debuting the plural today! It was not at all on purpose — that entry is made up of crossword-friendly letters, and it's really wordplay-friendly for cluing.

The NE and SW corners are my favorite parts of the grid. And, here's an alternative clue for 32A: Neuropsychological trait that may make you feel blue?

Jeff Chen notes:
So many debut entries today! DATA CENTERS was my favorite because of its innocent clue. I attended many networking events in my ... read more

So many debut entries today! DATA CENTERS was my favorite because of its innocent clue. I attended many networking events in my previous startup life. These days, I might enjoy much more nerdy networking parties involving not human, but computer servers.

The pandemic might have made me just a tad antisocial.

I love that Erica didn't stick to a standard "stair stack" of DANCE AROUND / MEME ACCOUNT / SYNESTHESIA, but wove a second one through it. With WILLA CATHER / LOOSE CHANGE / DATA CENTERS, six long marquee entries anchors an impressive center.

Was it just me who saw ME! ME! ACCOUNT as something overzealous people put together?

This is why I stay off social media these days.

What a great clue for VILEST! It's an entry that sounds slightly off — I would likely say "most vile" instead — but a memorable Oscar Wilde quote more than saves it.

I had a rough go with SKORTS / CARLO, weighing SKIRTS / CARLI for ages, especially since CARLI Lloyd is such a famous soccer star. Do golfers not wear SKIRTS anymore? Thankfully, I avoided the I/O error.

There was less ambiguity with SEA-GOAT / SYNESTHESIA. I was 95% sure that intersection had to be an S, but being a Capricorn and never having heard the term SEA-GOAT, I had a tough time seeing it as correct. It is definitely legit, as it turns out.

As much as I used to love LOLCATs, are they still mews-worthy?

Impressively wide-open center in this 66-worder.
